Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its creamy and rich flavor. It features al dente spaghetti tossed in a silky sauce made from eggs, Parmesan cheese, and crispy pancetta or bacon. A touch of garlic adds depth to the dish, making it a comforting and satisfying meal. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion, this recipe brings the authentic taste of Italy to your table.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Reserve a cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large pan, heat a bit of olive oil and cook the pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. Add minced garlic and saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  4. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with pancetta and garlic. Toss to combine.
  6. Remove the pan from heat and quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring constantly. Add reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with chopped parsley and more grated Parmesan, if desired.
