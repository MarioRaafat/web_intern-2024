Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate Chip Cookies are a classic treat loved by many. These cookies are perfectly crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside, filled with gooey chocolate chips in every bite. They are easy to make and perfect for any occasion, whether you’re baking for a party, a gift, or just because you want something sweet. Enjoy them fresh out of the oven or with a glass of milk.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and add to the batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts (if using).
  6. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
