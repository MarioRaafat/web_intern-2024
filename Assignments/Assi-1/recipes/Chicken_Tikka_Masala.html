Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish known for its flavorful and creamy tomato-based sauce. Marinated chicken pieces are cooked until tender and then simmered in a rich sauce made with tomatoes, cream, and aromatic spices. This dish is perfect for those who enjoy a bit of spice and warmth in their meals. Serve it with rice or naan for a complete and satisfying dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 can (400g) tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, paprika, and salt. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until no longer pink on the outside.
  5. Pour in the tomato puree and simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for another 5-10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
