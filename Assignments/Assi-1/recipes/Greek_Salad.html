Greek Salad

Greek Salad is a refreshing and healthy dish that combines fresh vegetables with tangy feta cheese and Kalamata olives. It's dressed with a simple vinaigrette made from olive oil, red wine vinegar, and oregano, making it a perfect side dish or light meal. This salad is vibrant, full of flavor, and easy to prepare, offering a taste of the Mediterranean in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 200g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, bell pepper, and olives.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Top with crumbled feta cheese.
  5. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator for an hour before serving.
